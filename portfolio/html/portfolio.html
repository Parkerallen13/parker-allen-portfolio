Hello, my name is Parker Allen. I'm a Creative Technology and Design student at CU Boulder who loves finding creative ways to bring ideas to life. As the Vice President of Marketing for Kappa Theta Pi, CU’s premier technology fraternity, and a member of CU Hyperloop’s marketing team, I’ve had the chance to work on style guides, and visual design for promotional materials. Recently, I’ve started exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to expand my skill set.

When I’m not working on projects, you’ll probably find me painting, drawing, or taking photos with my camera. Art and design are at the heart of everything I do, and I’m excited to grow into a career as a UI/UX designer, where I can combine my love for creativity and technical skills to build meaningful experiences.

I’d love to hear from you, so don’t hesitate to reach out with any questions or just to say hi!

Hello, I’m Parker Allen, a Creative Technology and Design student at CU Boulder. I currently serve as the Vice President of Marketing for Kappa Theta Pi, CU's premier technology fraternity, and contribute to the marketing team at CU Hyperloop. In these roles, I’ve developed expertise in creating style guides and promotional materials, while also expanding my technical skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Outside of my academic and extracurricular commitments, I pursue painting, drawing, and photography, which enhance my creative process and design approach. I aim to build a career in UI/UX design, where I can combine creativity and technical skills to create impactful user experiences.

Feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect. I look forward to hearing from you!

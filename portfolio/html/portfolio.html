Hello, my name is Parker Allen, and I am a Creative Technology and Design student at CU Boulder with a passion for bringing ideas to life through innovative design and technology. As the Vice President of Marketing for Kappa Theta Pi, CU’s premier technology fraternity, and a member of CU Hyperloop’s marketing team, I have honed my skills in crafting style guides and creating visually compelling promotional materials. Recently, I have been expanding my expertise by exploring HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to further enhance my technical capabilities.

Outside of my academic and extracurricular pursuits, I enjoy painting, drawing, and photography, which fuel my creativity and inform my design approach. My ultimate goal is to build a career in UI/UX design, where I can blend artistic vision with technical proficiency to create meaningful and impactful user experiences.

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or simply want to connect—I’d love to hear from you!
